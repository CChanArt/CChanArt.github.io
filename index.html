About Us

About Rinoa (Click to expand)

Our partnership began in 2010 when Rinoa was purchased from a breeding farm in Langley, British Columbia.
Born on June 20th 2007, Rinoa has been solely trained & ridden by yours truly. Standing at 17.0 hands tall, she's been jumped up to 1.50m and spent most of her life competing in Eventing, having reached Preliminary level in 2017. Her competition history includes Show Jumping, Hunters, and Lvl.3 Dressage. She loves treats, cross-country, sleeping (a lot!), and getting her neck scratched.

Sired by Rio Grande (x Pajazzo), Rinoa's physical traits are inspired by him with her bright bay colour, wild mane, blaze, rear stockings and front socks. Being a registered Canadian Warmblood (Hanoverian x Westphalian), she is very quiet with a temperament of 3. With her incredible mind, floating, comfortable gaits & scopey jump, Rinoa has proven herself to be a highly capable performance mare with great potential.

About Chris (Click to expand)

Before I could even walk, I was already riding horses (with my parents, of course!). Since then I've accumulated over 20 years of riding experience under my belt and have been competing across North America every year. Throughout my career I have raised ridden and trained several of my own horses as well as my clients', including problem horses and others which went on to be successful Hunters and Jumpers. My main specialty in training / competitions is Eventing (Preliminary) and Dressage (Fourth Level), but I have also competed in the Hunter ring as well.

In addition to training horses I have coached and taught at riding schools throughout Canada and the UK for several years, with students as young as age 3 in all aspects of English riding, horse care, and management. I've also coached at low-level events, and prepared students for competitions. Currently, I'm looking to secure my certification as a registered instructor through Equine Canada.

My credentials include Stage 3 (Coach) in Complete Horsemanship (Riding and Equine Care) and Groom Certificate with the British Horse Society, as well as a National Diploma in Equine Science and Management. I'm a certified stable manager, groom and athlete, and - if you haven't guessed already - an avid equestrian enthusiast aspiring to make it into the Canadian Equestrian Team and represent Canada in the 2020 Summer Olympics 3-Day Eventing.
