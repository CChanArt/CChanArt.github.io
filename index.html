About Us

Our partnership began when Rinoa was purchased from a breeding farm in Langley, British Columbia.
Born in 2007, Rinoa has been solely trained & ridden by yours truly. Standing at a tall 17.0hh, she's been jumped up to 1.50m and spent most of her life competing in Eventing, having reached Preliminary level in 2017. Her competition history includes Show Jumping, Hunters, and Lvl.3 Dressage. She loves treats, sleeping (a lot!), and getting her neck scratched.

Sired by Rio Grande (x Pajazzo), Rinoa's physical traits are inspired by his with her bright bay colour, blaze, rear stockings and front socks. Being a registered Canadian Warmblood (Hanoverian x Westphalian), she is very quiet with a temperament of around 3. With her incredible mind, combined with her floating, comfortable gaits & scopey jump, Rinoa has proven herself as a highly capable performance horse with great potential. (Rinoa's Family Tree)
